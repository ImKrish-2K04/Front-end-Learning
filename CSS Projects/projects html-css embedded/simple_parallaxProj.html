Phishing Attack

Phishing attacks are a common form of social engineering where attackers send fraudulent emails or messages that appear to be from a trusted source.

Trojan Horse

Trojan horse, or Trojan, is a type of malicious code or software that can take control of your computer.

Ransomware

Ransomware is malware which encrypts your files until you pay a ransom to the hackers.
